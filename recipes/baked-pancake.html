Baked Pancake

Description

Oven-baked pancakes, or Dutch babies, are the perfect choice for a delicious meal. Their light, fluffy texture, combined with the ease of preparation, makes it my go-to for lazy mornings or special brunches.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ lemon, juiced (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar, or to taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Place butter in a 9-inch pie dish. Place in the preheating oven until butter is melted, 5 to 10 minutes
  3. Meanwhile, whisk together flour, milk, eggs, white sugar, and salt in a bowl. Pour mixture into butter in dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges are light brown, and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 24 minutes.Bake in the preheated oven until the edges are light brown, and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 24 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle lemon juice over pancake and dust with confectioners' sugar. Serve warm.Sprinkle lemon juice over pancake and dust with confectioners' sugar. Serve warm.
